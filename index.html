About Us

Independent NVOCC & International Trading company with integrated freight forwarding, sourcing and consolidation solutions.

Hafen Consolidators

Hafen Consolidators is an independent NVOCC & International Trading company headquartered in Guayaquil, Ecuador. We provide integrated freight forwarding, sourcing, consolidation and cross-trade logistics solutions for global businesses — focused on micro, small and medium enterprises seeking reliable, cost-effective trade routes.

Core capabilities: Consolidation, cross-trade routing, customs pre-clearance, bonded warehousing and supplier consolidation.

Our Approach

We combine hands-on operations experience with technology-driven planning — route optimisation, transparent documentation and trusted global partnerships — to reduce lead times and total landed cost while improving service reliability.

Our promise: practical, measurable improvements for customers trading internationally.
